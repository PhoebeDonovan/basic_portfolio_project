About Phe & Rogs

Rogs and I have been together for many years now but our relationship was fairly new when we had the crazy idea to hike in th footsteps of Sir Edmond Hilary. The year was 2017 and we were setting out on the adventure of a lifetime!! Our first ever hiking holiday and in Nepal of all places.

Rogs and I had never done any serious hiking, sure we went on walks here and there but only a few hours at a time and never with a day bag let alone overnight stays! But we were young and full of adventurous spirit. So on a whim we decided we were going to hike to base camp on the Nepalese side of Mt Everest. Why not?

We spent 6 months or so training with various forms of cardio, endurance and strength training. I started eating vegan to account for the food we would have available to us (as well as for ethical and health reasons - still going strong today). We kitted ourselves out with hiking bags, sleeping bags, appropriate clothing, hiking packs, medication in case of infections or altitude sickness - you name it we had it. We decided to hike unguided, something which I was nervouse about seeing as I have virtually no sense of direction but I trusted Rogs navigational skills and rightfully so!

Future thinking

Although we loved being indepent on our hike we met some amazing guides along the way and we look forward to employing their services for our next Nepalese hiking adventure! Their knowledge of the areas history and culture adds even more colour to the yourney as well as getting to enjoy their commpany, exchanging stories and playing local games in the evenings was a highlight for us.
